About Us

MINJUKIM is a Seoul-based fashion brand founded by designer Minju Kim in 2015. Known for blending playful, youthful designs with avant-garde couture, the brand's collections are characterized by bold silhouettes, feminine elements, and a whimsical aesthetic. Minju Kim gained international recognition after winning Netflix's Next in Fashion and has previously been a semi-finalist in the LVMH Prize and winner of the H&M Design Awards


The official online shop offers a variety of categories, including outerwear, dresses, tops, bottoms, and accessories. Shoppers can also find sale items and limited-edition pieces.
